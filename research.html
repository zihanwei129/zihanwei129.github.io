Abstract: Applying computational fluid dynamics to model magma flow in volcanic conduits and bridge magma flow with various observational data. The goal is to understand volcanic degassing processes and eruption dynamics.



Optimizing Contact Tracing Policies for COVID-19

Abstract: We use agent-based modeling to optimize contact tracing policies for COVID-19 in San Francisco, CA. We focus on the effect different types of contact tracing, and different work types. This work is part of the COVID-19 project of Stanford Future Bay Initiative. Read more

Quantifying Groundwater Storage in Central Valley, California Using GRACE Data and Head Measurements

Abstract: I study the groundwater storage change in California's Central Valley during 2002-2017 using the GRACE data and the in-situ well measurements. The recorded variations in groundwater level can also inform the aquifer properties.
